About Aplixo

Empowering students to discover and access educational opportunities across India

Our Mission

Aplixo is dedicated to making educational opportunities accessible to every student in India. We believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to quality education. Our platform aggregates scholarships, internships, and government schemes from verified sources, making it easier for students to discover opportunities they qualify for.

We don't accept applications directly or store sensitive personal information. Instead, we act as a trusted discovery platform that connects students with official opportunity providers, ensuring transparency and security throughout the process.
