Introduction

Welcome to Aplyra. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we handle information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection, use, and protection.

What We Are

Aplyra is a scholarship and opportunity discovery platform. We act as an aggregator and information portal, connecting students with official scholarship providers, internship programs, and government schemes.

Important: We do not accept applications, collect sensitive personal data, or process any scholarship/internship applications. All applications are completed directly on official provider websites.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Search Queries: When you search for opportunities, we temporarily process your search terms to deliver relevant results.
  • Filter Preferences: Your selected filters (course level, state, category) are used only during your session.

Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics about how users interact with our platform to improve our services.
  • Device Information: Basic technical information (browser type, device type, screen size) to optimize your experience.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies to maintain your session and preferences during your visit.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification documents (Aadhaar, PAN, etc.)
  • Financial information (bank details, income certificates)
  • Educational certificates or marksheets
  • Application forms or submission data
  • Login credentials (we have no authentication system in v1)

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used to:

  • Provide search and filtering functionality
  • Display relevant opportunities based on your criteria
  • Improve our platform's performance and user experience
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services
  • Ensure platform security and prevent abuse

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties. When you click "Apply on Official Website," you are redirected to the scholarship provider's website where their own privacy policy applies.

We may share anonymous, aggregated data (e.g., "500 users searched for engineering scholarships this month") for research or reporting purposes.

Data Storage and Security

Since we don't collect sensitive personal information, our data storage requirements are minimal. Any data we do store is:

  • Stored securely using industry-standard practices
  • Protected against unauthorized access
  • Retained only as long as necessary for our services
  • Automatically deleted after your session ends (for temporary data)

Third-Party Links

Aplyra contains links to external websites (scholarship providers, government portals, educational institutions). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before submitting any personal information.

Children's Privacy

Aplyra is designed for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If we discover we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any information we have about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics tracking
  • Clear cookies and browsing data at any time

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our website or email us at privacy@aplyra.in
